How to use Alino

Set up in under
two minutes.

No technical knowledge needed. From box to working in four simple steps.

1
🔋

Charge your device

Connect Alino to power using any USB-C cable. A full charge takes about one hour and gives you a full day of use. Check the battery level in the Alino app anytime.

💡 Charge it overnight before your first use
2
📱

Get the Alino app

Scan the QR code included in the box to install the Alino app on your Android phone. The app is essential — it detects your posture, shows battery level, and provides analytics. Open the app, create your account, and pair your device via Bluetooth in under a minute.

💡 The app is required to use Alino — keep Bluetooth turned on
3
👕

Pin it on and calibrate

Pin Alino onto any clothing you're wearing at the upper back area near the base of your neck. Sit in your most comfortable upright position, then press and hold the button for two seconds. Done — it has learned your baseline.

💡 Recalibrate if you switch chairs or sitting positions
4
🚀

Go back to your day

That's it. Study, work, or game as normal. Alino monitors your posture silently. The moment you slouch, you feel a vibration — and the app sends a notification and logs the session data for your analytics.

💡 Check the app at the end of each day to track your progress
